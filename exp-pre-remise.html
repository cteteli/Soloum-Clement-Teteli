Monitoring & Evaluation – PRE REMISE-Benin Project, NGO SAVE OUR PLANET, Parakou

Period: January & May 2023 Location: Parakou, Benin Organisation: NGO SAVE OUR PLANET Type: Environmental monitoring & evaluation

Mission Description

The PRE REMISE-Benin project (Reforestation and Restoration of Sensitive Environments in Benin) is an initiative by NGO SAVE OUR PLANET in the Parakou region. I carried out monitoring and evaluation missions in January and May 2023, covering the supervision of planting activities, field data collection, environmental impact assessment and coordination with local stakeholders.

  • Supervision of reforestation and restoration activities in sensitive environments in the Parakou region
  • Collection and analysis of ecological monitoring data (seedling survival rates, vegetation cover evolution)
  • Environmental impact assessment of activities on target ecosystems and neighbouring communities
  • Organisation and facilitation of environmental awareness sessions for local populations
  • Coordination of stakeholders (farmers, women's groups, local authorities) for activity implementation
  • Writing technical monitoring reports submitted to the NGO and funding partners
  • Photographic and cartographic documentation of reforestation sites and observed progress

Skills Developed

Mastery of monitoring-evaluation methodologies for environmental projects; collection and analysis of ecological field data; facilitation of participatory awareness sessions; writing monitoring reports for donors; multi-stakeholder coordination in an NGO context.
