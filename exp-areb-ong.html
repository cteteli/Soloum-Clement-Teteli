Municipal Coordinator – NGO AREB-BENIN, Ouaké, Benin

Period: March 2023 – Present Location: Ouaké, Benin Organisation: NGO AREB-BENIN Type: NGO Coordination

Role Description

As Municipal Coordinator of NGO AREB-BENIN in the municipality of Ouaké, I ensure the institutional representation of the organisation, management of local teams and implementation of conservation, sustainable agriculture and environmental education projects in the field. This position involves close coordination with local authorities, communities and technical partners.

  • Representing NGO AREB-BENIN with local authorities, communities and partners in the municipality of Ouaké
  • Planning, supervision and monitoring of field activities related to biodiversity conservation and sustainable agriculture
  • Management and coordination of local volunteer and field technician teams
  • Organisation of environmental awareness campaigns for rural populations
  • Writing progress reports, technical notes and meeting minutes for NGO headquarters
  • Coordination with institutional stakeholders (town hall, forestry service, farmer groups) for integration of activities into local development plans
  • Participatory monitoring and evaluation of activities through performance indicators defined with beneficiaries

Skills Developed

Team leadership and project management in an NGO context; intercultural communication and community engagement; strategic planning and monitoring-evaluation; institutional report writing; multi-stakeholder coordination; mastery of participatory approaches to sustainable local development.
