Vice-President – AUF Club "Environmental Defenders", Kinshasa, DRC

Period: December 2021 – December 2022 Location: Kinshasa, DRC Organisation: AUF Club / Agence Universitaire de la Francophonie Type: Student association engagement

Role Description

The AUF Club "Environmental Defenders" is a student initiative affiliated with the Agence Universitaire de la Francophonie (AUF) based in Kinshasa, Democratic Republic of Congo. As Vice-President, I co-led the club's activities focused on waste management awareness, recycling and the promotion of a healthy urban environment. This one-year mandate allowed me to develop my leadership, communication and environmental event organisation skills.

  • Co-leadership of AUF Club "Environmental Defenders" activities in Kinshasa with the Executive Board
  • Organisation of workshops on waste sorting and recycling for students and the general public
  • Design and facilitation of awareness campaigns on urban cleanliness and ecological waste management
  • Coordination of club members (scheduling, internal communication, task allocation)
  • Partnerships with other student associations and local institutions for joint environmental events
  • Representation of the club at official AUF events and academic gatherings
  • Writing activity reports and minutes submitted to AUF and university management

Skills Developed

Leadership and management of a student association; design and facilitation of environmental awareness campaigns; organisation of thematic workshops; institutional communication and representation; networking within the international Francophonie (AUF); civic engagement and environmental responsibility in urban settings.
