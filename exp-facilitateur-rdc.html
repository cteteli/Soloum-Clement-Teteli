Facilitator – European Union / Tshumba-kituti Project, Democratic Republic of Congo

Period: April 2022 Location: Eastern DRC (Tshumba-kituti) Funder: European Union Type: Field facilitation / Ecosystem services

Mission Description

As part of the EU-funded project in the Tshumba-kituti region (eastern Democratic Republic of Congo), I served as a field facilitator. The mission aimed to inventory local ecosystem services and raise awareness among rural communities on the sustainable management of natural resources, including forests, fungi and useful plant species. This field work allowed me to work directly at the interface between scientific teams and indigenous communities.

  • Participatory inventory of ecosystem services (provisioning, regulating, cultural) in Tshumba-kituti forests
  • Facilitation of exchanges between scientific teams and local communities (translation, cultural mediation)
  • Organisation of awareness workshops on sustainable management of forest and fungal resources
  • Collection of ethnobotanical and ethno-mycological data from indigenous populations
  • Participatory mapping of local natural resource uses (gathering areas, key species)
  • Contribution to capacity-building workshops on conservation and valorisation of Non-Timber Forest Products (NTFPs)
  • Writing field reports and inventory technical sheets submitted to project managers

Skills Developed

Intercultural facilitation and community mediation; participatory inventory of ecosystem services; collection of ethnobotanical and ethno-mycological data; facilitation of capacity-building workshops; technical report writing in an EU project context; work in African tropical forest environments.
