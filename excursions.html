Field Missions

Lama Forest, Benin

June 2023
Lama Forest, Benin

Fungal biodiversity inventory and intensive collection of mycorrhizal fungi as part of a doctoral project. Observation of interactions between forest species and fungi, data collection for a phylogenetic study and field training workshops with students and researchers from the University of Parakou.

Kinshasa & Tshumba-kituti, DRC

April 2022
Kinshasa  /  Tshumba-kituti, Democratic Republic of Congo

Community awareness mission on waste management and inventory of local ecosystem services within an EU-funded project. Recycling training, participatory survey of local populations, and data collection for integrating ecosystem services into local development plans.

Temperate Forests, Belgium

October 2024
Ghent, Belgium

Participation in practical mycology workshops in Belgian temperate forests: morphological identification of ectomycorrhizal fungi specimens, extraction and analysis of environmental DNA (eDNA), field activities with international students and researchers. Enhancement of skills in molecular biology, phylogenetics and fungal taxonomy.

Clément Soloum TETELI

PhD Student in Biology · Ghent University · University of Parakou
