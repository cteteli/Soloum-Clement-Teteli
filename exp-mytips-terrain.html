Field Agent – MyTIPS 2020 Project (Mycorrhizal Tropical Inoculum Production System), Benin

Period: September – October 2020 Location: Benin Project: MyTIPS Type: Field collection mission

Mission Description

The MyTIPS (Mycorrhizal Tropical Inoculum Production System) project is an applied research project on the production of mycorrhizal inoculums adapted to tropical conditions to sustainably improve agricultural productivity. As a field agent, I participated in soil sampling campaigns and farmer interviews across various agro-ecological zones of Benin, contributing to the project's database on mycorrhizal fungi diversity.

  • Rhizospheric soil and cultivated plant root sampling (maize, sorghum, yam) across multiple agro-ecological zones of Benin
  • Conducting structured interviews with farmers about their farming practices and use of biological inputs
  • Collection and packaging of soil samples for laboratory analysis (identification of mycorrhizal fungi)
  • Observation and description of visual mycorrhization symptoms on roots collected in situ
  • Entry and coding of field data in standardised MyTIPS project sheets
  • Contribution to preliminary field data analysis in collaboration with the laboratory team
  • Participation in MyTIPS team coordination meetings to monitor and adjust the collection protocol

Skills Developed

Soil and root sampling techniques in tropical conditions; conducting semi-structured interviews with farmers; macroscopic observation and identification of mycorrhizal symptoms; field data management and coding; teamwork in a multidisciplinary team (agronomists, biologists, socio-economists).
