Ecological and Sustainable Agriculture Practice (Nexus) – Songhaï Centre, Porto-Novo, Benin

Period: May 2023 Location: Songhaï Centre, Porto-Novo, Benin Type: Practical agroecology training

Course Description

The Songhaï Centre is a world-renowned integrated agroecological development centre based in Porto-Novo, Benin. The Nexus training programme offers practical immersion in ecological and sustainable agriculture, combining food production, energy and resource management in an integrated and circular approach. This training allowed me to connect my mycorrhizal fungi research to practical agroecological applications and sustainable food production in African tropical contexts.

  • Integrated agroecology: food-energy-water Nexus in sustainable production systems
  • Organic and ecological farming practices: compost, biopesticides, crop rotation
  • Integrated livestock and crop production systems in agroecological contexts
  • Aquaculture and agroforestry integrated into sustainable food production systems
  • Renewable energy management (biogas, solar) in agroecological farms
  • Entrepreneurship and economic viability of ecological agriculture in Africa
  • Role of soil fungi and beneficial microorganisms in sustainable farming systems

Skills Acquired

Mastery of integrated agroecological practices; understanding of the Nexus food-energy-water in tropical production systems; connections between mycorrhizal fungi research and sustainable agriculture; agroecological entrepreneurship skills; practical experience in an internationally recognised model agroecological farm.
