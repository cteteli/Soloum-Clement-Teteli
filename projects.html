Grant N° BOF-UGent/2024/01

Mycorrhizal Diversity and Function in Tropical Agroecosystems of West Africa

Period Jan 2024 – Dec 2027
Funder Ghent University (BOF) & FWO Belgium
Location Benin & Belgium
Type PhD Research

Objective: To characterize the diversity, distribution, and ecological functions of ectomycorrhizal and arbuscular mycorrhizal fungi in tropical agroecosystems across Benin (West Africa), using morphological, molecular (eDNA, barcoding), and phylogenetic approaches, in order to develop sustainable inoculation strategies for forest restoration.

Mycology Agroecosystems eDNA Forest Restoration

Grant N° ISRA/LNRPV/2025-02

Mycorrhiza Culture and Fungal Inoculum Production for Tree Restoration in Senegal

Period Mar 2025 – May 2026
Funder ISRA – Senegalese Institute of Agricultural Research
Location Dakar, Senegal
Type Applied Research / Training

Objective: To develop and optimize mycorrhizal inoculum production protocols for native tree species at the National Laboratory for Plant Production Research (LNRPV), aiming to strengthen the restoration strategy of degraded ecosystems in the Sahel region through low-cost, locally adaptable biotechnological solutions.

MyTIPS – Mycorrhizal Tropical Inoculum Production System (Benin)
