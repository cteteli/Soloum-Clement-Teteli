MOOC PAPACO: Conservation of Protected Areas in Africa – PAPACO / Université Senghor

Period: June 2022 Institution: PAPACO / Université Senghor Type: MOOC (online course)

Course Description

The PAPACO MOOC (Programme for Protected Areas in Africa) offered by PAPACO and Université Senghor provides comprehensive online training on the management and conservation of protected areas in Africa. This course strengthened my understanding of biodiversity conservation frameworks, protected area governance and management tools, which are directly relevant to my research on the conservation of fungal biodiversity in tropical African ecosystems.

  • International frameworks for protected area conservation: CBD, Aichi Targets, Kunming-Montreal GBF
  • Governance of African protected areas: types, stakeholders, decision-making processes
  • Management planning: conservation objectives, zoning, adaptive management
  • Monitoring and evaluation of protected area management effectiveness (METT)
  • Human-wildlife conflict management and engagement of local communities
  • Financing of protected areas in Africa: tourism, PES, international funds
  • Biodiversity of African protected areas: fauna, flora and soil microorganisms

Skills Acquired

Understanding of international biodiversity conservation frameworks; protected area governance and management; monitoring-evaluation of management effectiveness; integration of local communities in conservation; financing tools for protected areas; understanding of African biodiversity including fungal biodiversity.
