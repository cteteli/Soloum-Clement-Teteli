Training in Environmental Economics and Natural Resource Management – IFDD (OIF) & Université Senghor

Period: March 2022 Institution: IFDD (OIF) & Université Senghor Type: Online specialised training

Course Description

This training in environmental economics and natural resource management, offered by IFDD (OIF) in partnership with Université Senghor, provides economic concepts and analytical tools for the valuation of natural resources and ecosystems, essential for sustainable environmental management in developing countries. This economic perspective complements my scientific expertise in mycology and allows me to better articulate the economic value of mycorrhizal fungi for agriculture and biodiversity.

  • Fundamentals of environmental economics: market failures, externalities, public goods
  • Valuation of natural resources and ecosystems: contingent valuation, hedonic pricing, cost-benefit analysis
  • Ecosystem services economics: TEEB, natural capital accounting, green GDP
  • Economic instruments for environmental policy: taxes, subsidies, tradable permits, PES
  • Economics of natural resource management: sustainable yield, depletion, intergenerational equity
  • Green economy and sustainable development: green finance, impact investment, circular economy
  • Economic valuation of fungal biodiversity and mycorrhizal ecosystem services in Africa

Skills Acquired

Understanding of environmental economics concepts; ecosystem services economic valuation methods; economic instruments for sustainable natural resource management; green economy and natural capital accounting; ability to articulate the economic value of mycorrhizal fungi for sustainable agriculture and biodiversity conservation.
