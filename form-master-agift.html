Master AGIFT – Agroforestry, Integrated Management of Tropical Forests – ERAIFT-UNESCO, Kinshasa, DRC

Period: 2021 – 2023 Institution: ERAIFT-UNESCO Location: Kinshasa, DRC Degree: International Master's

Programme Overview

The AGIFT Master's (Agroforestry and Integrated Management of Tropical Forests) is a two-year international training programme offered by ERAIFT (Regional Postgraduate School for Integrated Management of Tropical Forests and Territories), a UNESCO category 2 centre based in Kinshasa, DRC. This programme allowed me to acquire advanced skills in integrated tropical forest management, agroforestry and sustainable biodiversity management at the African regional scale.

  • In-depth training in tropical agroforestry: design, establishment and monitoring of sustainable agroforestry systems
  • Integrated management of tropical forests: forest planning, governance, management plans, zoning
  • Ecology and biodiversity of African tropical forests: floristic inventories, ecosystem services, conservation
  • Environmental and social impact assessment (ESIA) of development projects in forest areas
  • Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and remote sensing applied to forest resource management
  • Practical field work in tropical forests of Central Africa (DRC, Congo)
  • Master's thesis on integrated management of land and natural resources in tropical environments

Skills Acquired

Expertise in tropical agroforestry and integrated forest management; mastery of GIS and remote sensing tools for forest management; skills in environmental impact assessment; research capabilities in tropical forest ecology; African and international professional network in natural resource management.
